Are you looking into joining a scout troop?
Troop 6059 meets every Tuesday evening from 7:00 pm until 8:30 pm. We currently do not have a set location.
However, we usually meet at the back of Access Academy on 1500 N. Mississippi St. in the Cafeteria. (formerly known as Kenner Hall). As of this moment, we are currently meeting at First Christian Church on Taylor Loop.
We have resumed our weekly meetings, and we sincerely apologize for the lack of updates on our social media platforms, we apologize for any inconvience caused. Furthermore, we encourage our members, and those looking to join to stay tuned to our various social media platforms, and in addition to this staying tuned for our emails.

Troop 59 and First Christian Church are proud to also sponsor Cub Pack 57. For more information, about Pack 57, Please visit the pack website or email our Scoutmaster Brad Chilcote at bradc@wddarchitects.com or call 501-425-3940.
